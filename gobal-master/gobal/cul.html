Language



Within the United Arab Emirates the language spoken is Arabic ( but also English). Arabic is the largest member of the Semitic language family. There are 28 letters in arabic, all written in a script like form. When writing in arabic you go from right to left.

Religion

The United Arab Emirates are mostly Muslim. More than 85 percent are Sunni Muslim and an estimated 15 percent or fewer are Shia Muslims.There are other religions people practice within the UAE like: hindu, christans, bubbhist, Parsi, Bahaiand Sikh.
